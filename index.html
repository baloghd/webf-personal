My favorite Latin quotes

I have always liked the profoundness of Latin quotes.

I have studied a whole host of languages to get a feel for them (German, Spanish, French, Romanian, Russian besides English), but I never formally studied Latin.
Some of my favourites include: Per aspera ad astra, which roughly translates to "through hardships to the stars". In the same vein, I also like Dum spiro, spero, which means "while I breathe, I hope". All in all, my absolute favorite one is Age quod agis, which equals "if you do something, do it well".
